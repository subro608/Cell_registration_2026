## Axis Assignments — CONFIRMED by Erdem (2026-04-05)

| Volume axis | Anatomical | Direction (positive end) | Confirmed |
|-------------|-----------|--------------------------|-----------|
| Z (depth, row1→row5) | **DV** (dorsal→ventral) | Row1 = dorsal (oriens), Row5 = ventral (pyramidale) | Yes |
| X (horizontal in slice) | **ML** (medial-lateral) | Left = M, Right = L | Yes |
| Y (vertical in slice) | **AP** (anterior-posterior) | Top = A, Bottom = P | Yes |

**Additional context from Erdem:**
- Row 1 = most dorsal (stratum oriens)
- Row 5 = most ventral (stratum pyramidale starts around row 3_5/4_1)
- Top of each tile = anterior, bottom = posterior
- Left = medial, right = lateral

**How to apply:** Scene7 3-axis widget labels are now confirmed correct: ML (X), AP (Y), DV (Z). Arrow tips point to L, A, V respectively.
